🔥 Chest Training Tips

• Beginners: 3 sets per exercise, 8-12 reps.
• Intermediate: 4 sets, include incline + dips.
• Advanced: heavy bench + drop sets + slow negatives.

💡 Tip: Always control the weight and squeeze at the top.
